### Javascript If you don't know any Javascript or are just beginning, don't worry. There are many great resources to get started and while it might seem hard at first you'll see that you'll get better quickly. It's also good to know that Javascript is worth learning even apart from Framer, as you can do web, application and backend development with it. Most people agree it's a language with a future.
